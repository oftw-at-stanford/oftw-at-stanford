How to Get Involved

  1. Take the Pledge

    By donating a small percentage of your income to the most effective charities, you can have an incredible direct impact on the world.

  2. Become an ambassador

    Once you've taken the pledge, spread the word! Becoming an ambassador lets you help others learn about One for the World and effective ways to combat world issues, and it gives you access to an amazing and supportive community of those motivated to do good.

  3. Attend events, make lifelong connections

    Take a look at our upcoming events! Participate, have fun, and meet new friends.
